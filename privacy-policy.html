Privacy Policy

Muslim Prayer Time: Kaaba & Zikr · Last updated: June 2026

This privacy policy explains how Muslim Prayer Time: Kaaba & Zikr processes your data. Most of your data stays on your device.

1. Developer and Contact

Developer / publisher: Muslim Prayer Time: Kaaba & Zikr. For privacy questions or requests, contact us at umrahhajidigital@gmail.com.

2. Data We Process

  • Location: Used for prayer times, qibla direction, city detection, and the mosque finder. Prayer time and qibla calculations are performed on your device. Your location is not stored on our servers.
  • Step count: Used for step goals, progress, and in-app rewards. It is not used for medical diagnosis or health treatment.
  • Prayer, dhikr, tasbih, favorites, coins, XP, badges, and settings: Stored on your device so the app can show your progress and preferences.
  • Profile data (optional): If you add a profile name, bio, avatar, cover image, gallery items, or profile preferences, this data is stored on your device. Selected photos are not uploaded to our servers.
  • Country leaderboard: Only your country code/country name and dhikr totals are processed for the country leaderboard. The leaderboard is country-based and does not publish your personal identity.
  • Google account (optional): If you choose Google sign-in for backup/restore, your Google account name and email address may be used to show the signed-in account and to access your private Google Drive app data space.
  • Google Drive backup (optional): If you use backup/restore, progress data such as coins, XP, profile preferences, unlocked items, badges, dhikr totals, and favorites may be stored in your own private Google Drive app data folder.
  • Manual backup/export (optional): If you manually export a backup file, the JSON file may include selected progress data and your device model. You choose where to share or store that file.
  • Advertising identifier and device information: Used by Google AdMob to serve ads and help protect against abuse.

3. Permissions

Location, activity recognition (step counter), notification, photo/media, alarm, and internet permissions are requested only to run the related features. Photo/media access is used only when you choose a profile image or save/download an image. Notification and alarm permissions are used for prayer time, adhan, dhikr, and reminder notifications.

4. Third-Party Services

The app works with trusted service providers for limited functions:

  • To find your city name and nearby mosques, your approximate/current location may be sent to map data services based on OpenStreetMap, such as Overpass API mirrors.
  • Country leaderboard data is stored in Google Firebase / Google Cloud.
  • Optional backup is kept in your own private Google Drive app data space. We do not operate a server that can browse your Drive files.
  • Ads are served through Google AdMob.
  • Google sign-in is used only if you choose backup/restore.

5. Ads

The app shows banner, interstitial, and rewarded ads through Google AdMob. AdMob may process your advertising identifier and device information to serve ads. Rewarded ads are optional, and some sections of the app are ad-free.

6. Data Sharing and Sale

Data is shared only with the relevant service providers so that the features above can work. We do not sell personal and sensitive user data.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

Data stored on your device remains there until you delete it. You can erase local app data at any time using the "Reset Data" option in the app's settings, by uninstalling the app, or by clearing the app's data from your device settings.

The app does not create a separate account on our own servers. If you use Google backup, the backup is stored in your own Google Drive app data space. You can delete this backup from inside the app by going to Settings → Account and Data Deletion → Delete Drive Backup. You can also remove it from your Google Drive settings (Manage apps → delete app data). You can disconnect your Google account in the app's settings. For privacy requests, contact umrahhajidigital@gmail.com.

8. Security

Most of your data is kept on your device. When the app connects to the internet, it communicates over the standard secure (HTTPS) connections provided by the services listed above.

9. Children’s Privacy

This app is not specifically directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal data from them.

10. Changes and Contact

This policy may be updated from time to time; significant changes are announced in the app. For questions and privacy requests: umrahhajidigital@gmail.com
