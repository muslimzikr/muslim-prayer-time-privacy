Account and Data Deletion

Prayer Times, Qibla & Zikr / Muslim Prayer Time: Kaaba & Zikr · Last updated: June 2026

1. App and Developer

This page applies to the app shown on Google Play as Prayer Times, Qibla & Zikr, also identified as Muslim Prayer Time: Kaaba & Zikr. For deletion requests, contact: umrahhajidigital@gmail.com.

2. Does the App Create an Account?

The app does not create a separate account on our own servers. Google sign-in is optional and is used for backup/restore through the user's own private Google Drive app data space.

3. How to Delete Local App Data

  1. Open the app.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Use Reset Data to remove local progress data from your device.

You can also delete local data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data from your Android device settings.

4. How to Disconnect Google Sign-In

  1. Open the app.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Use the Google backup/sign-in section to disconnect your Google account, if connected.

5. How to Delete Google Drive Backup Data

If you used optional Google Drive backup, the backup is stored in your own Google Drive app data area. You can remove it from your Google account settings:

  1. Open Google Drive settings.
  2. Go to Manage apps.
  3. Find the app entry related to Prayer Times, Qibla & Zikr / Muslim Prayer Time.
  4. Choose the option to delete hidden app data or app data.

6. Request Deletion by Email

If you need help or want to request deletion support, email us at umrahhajidigital@gmail.com with the subject:

Data deletion request - Prayer Times, Qibla & Zikr

We may ask for reasonable information to understand your request. Do not send passwords, keystore files, private keys, or sensitive documents.

7. Data Deleted or Kept

When local data is reset, the app removes local progress and preference data stored on your device, such as coins, XP, dhikr progress, favorites, profile preferences, badges, unlocked items, and similar app progress data.

If Google Drive backup data is deleted from your Google account, backup data such as coins, XP, profile preferences, unlocked items, badges, dhikr totals, and favorites is removed from your private Drive app data space.

Some data may remain with third-party providers according to their own policies, such as Google AdMob advertising records, Firebase/Google Cloud operational logs, or OpenStreetMap/Overpass request logs. We do not operate a separate user account database for this app.

8. Retention Period

Local device data remains until you delete it. Google Drive backup data remains in your Google account until you delete it or disconnect/remove app data from Google Drive settings. Email deletion requests are normally reviewed within a reasonable period; we may retain request emails as support records where legally or operationally necessary.
